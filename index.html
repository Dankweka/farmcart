    About Us

    Farmcart is a local solution to small agricultural producers who are not in a position to buy and/ hire advanced farm machines like tractors and power tillers due to cost constraints. Most smallholder farmers are in rural areas, therefore they produce foods that satisfy only family consumption. Mostly they use local tools like hand hoes and oxen which are time consuming, with the farm cart technology farmers will be able to perform farm works with the same satisfactions as using other heavy machines like tractors at cheapest cost which is affordable to small holder farmers up to 50% cost reduction. The technology is performing the following activities: Planting, ploughing, water pump station(pumping), cargo (pulling up to 700kg at 18 degrees to 23 degrees inclination), boom spraying system, mowering, maize shelling system, farm transport (4 people sitting comfortably) and fertigation. The technology is firstly tested by Sokoine University of Agriculture farms in Morogoro, Tanzania In future, We will be the modern agricultural machinery manufacturing and exporting enterprise which integrates with research,development and production of feed processing,field management and plant protection,and agricultural tillage machinery from Tanzania for small scaled farmers. We will be supplying farms with quality agricultural machinery from Poultry Feed Processing Machinery,Self-propelled Boom Sprayers,Multi-Purpose Field Management Machinery,Seeding and Fertilizing Machines,Mini Slicers,Mini Power Tillers,Tillage implements and accessories,Cereal and Oilseed Processing Machinery,Rice Planters and Seeders,and Compound Fertilizers.

    Our Mission

    Through intensive researches, direct farmers field schools in the adaptation of new technologies and practices from our enterprise, support local innovation that will aim in solving farmers' machinery problem and collaborate with other basic partners, institutions (Non-government and government) in promoting the farm machinery integration to small scale farmers.

    Our Vision

    A leading enterprise in the manufacture and distribution of quality agricultural implements to smallholder farmers at affordable prices.
